# OEP-0000: Replace with proposal title

This file is the OEP template. Copy it to the number assigned by the steward and
replace every placeholder. Delete instructional prose that does not form part
of the proposal.

## Abstract

State the decision and externally observable result in a few paragraphs.

## Motivation

Describe the problem, users, evidence, and why a normative decision is needed.

## Scope and non-goals

Name what changes, what does not, and which future work remains impossible.

## Specification

Define behavior precisely enough for independent implementations and tests.
Use normative terms consistently and identify every versioned input.

## Alternatives

Record credible alternatives, evaluation criteria, reproducible results,
reasons for rejection, and evidence that would cause reconsideration.

## Compatibility and migration

Cover source, binary, schema, evidence, package, operational, and ecosystem
compatibility; include a bounded migration and downgrade/rollback story.

## Semantic and claim effects

List changed semantics, public claim families, claim scope, assumptions,
statuses, non-claims, and composition behavior. Explain `No change` claims.

## TCB, axiom, and proof effects

Show the old and new trust closure, axioms, checker obligations, certificates,
and independent replay consequences.

## Threat, abuse, and leakage effects

Link stable threat IDs, enumerate abuse paths and attacker capability changes,
state leakage-model effects, and record residual risk.

## Target and ABI effects

Identify affected hosts, targets, object formats, CPU features, calling
conventions, foreign boundaries, and unsupported behavior.

## Standards, errata, and provenance

Cite primary standards and errata with exact editions, dates, clauses, source
terms, archival locations, and transcription/intent risks.

## Dependencies, licenses, and IP

Give complete direct/transitive effects, immutable identities, licenses,
patents, contribution terms, export constraints, and removal plans.

## Conformance, tests, and evidence

Define positive, negative, adversarial, differential, and replay cases plus the
evidence and exit criteria that can falsify this proposal.

## Operations, release, and recovery

Cover CI, deployment, monitoring, key and update handling, rollback, revocation,
disaster recovery, and required release ceremony changes.

## Support and deprecation

State supported tuples, maintenance period, withdrawal triggers, migration
assistance, and downstream notification.

## Unresolved questions

List every remaining choice and the evidence/authority required to close it.

## Decision record

Record review dates, reviewers and recusals, authority disposition, rationale,
dissent, conditions, and the coupled decision-register changes. Leave this
section incomplete while the status is Draft or Review.
